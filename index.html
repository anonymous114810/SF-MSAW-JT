Audio Watermarking Demo

Supplementary Material (Anonymous)

Abstract

This demo illustrates a separation-first, multi-stream audio watermarking framework. Three methods are shown: Baseline, Jointly Trained, and Finetuned. Each method embeds watermarks in two stems (Accomp / Vocal), then mixes, separates, and decodes the audio.
