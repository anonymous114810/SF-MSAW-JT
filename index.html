Audio Watermarking and Source Separation: Performance Demonstration

Supplementary Material for Research Paper (Anonymous)

Abstract

This demo illustrates a separation-first, multi-stream audio watermarking framework. We embed distinct watermarks into multiple audio stems, mix them, perform source separation, and decode the watermarks. Baseline methods may fail to recover watermarks after separation, while our jointly trained approach demonstrates high post-separation decoding accuracy and good perceptual quality.
